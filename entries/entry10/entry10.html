SMELL OF DENIM JACKET

Some denim items have a very interesting or even strange smell, especially when they're new. I couldn't pinpoint exactly what it smells like, but it has a distinct artificial and chemical odor that may come from the dyeing or manufacturing process. I honestly don't have a strong preference for or against the smell, but it always reminds me of my dad, who is a (sort of) huge denim lover.

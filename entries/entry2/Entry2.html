Chloe Cedrus

To me, Chloe Cedrus is a perfume of floral and woody scent combination. It has a very elegant, relaxing, and tender quality in it and it is perfect for fall. It reminded me on a damp day in a forest.

Fragrance Notes:

Fragrance Family: Earthy & Woody, Floral, Warm & Spicy

Top: Subtle Woods, Smoky
Middle: Balsam
Base: Musky

Ingredients: Alcohol Denat., Parfum/Fragrance, Aqua/Water/Eau, Limonene, Ethylhexyl Methoxycinnamate, Linalool, Hydroxycitronellal, Benzophenone-3, Ethylhexyl Salicylate, Citronellol, Alpha-Isomethyl Ionone, Butyl Methoxydibenzoylmethane, Coumarin, Citral, Eugenol, Isoeugenol, Geraniol, Farnesol, Cinnamal, Bht, Acrylates/Octylacrylamide Copolymer, Hydrolyzed Jojoba Esters.

(Credit to Sephora and Costco)


Brought Me Back to Singapore

Last summer, I went to Singapore with one of my best and oldest friend, Meagan, where I bought this bottle of perfume from Chloe. It becomes my favourite fragrance and every time when I apply it, it brought me back to my trip to Singapore.

A more specific moment I recalled from the trip whenever I notice or apply this perfume is the day we went shopping. It was day two of the trip and we visited a shopping mall to find another perfume that I have been looking for for a very long time. But we accidentally came across this one in the Sephora in Singapore, and when I tested it on my wrist, I immediately fell in love with it.

NEW YORK PIZZA

The smell of Pizza reminded me of the recollections of NYC evenings in NYC and the feeling of contentment

NYC PIZZA

Pizza is now one of my comfort food I would get whenever I feel stressed and needed a little bit of treat in New York. It smells like a delicious mix of fresh-baked bread, tomato sauce with herbs, melting cheese, and the unique aroma of different toppings. The smell of a freshly baked pizza gives a sense of comforting, especially in a winter evening. Something special about New York-style pizza is its thin and foldable crust and its large slice that always serves on a white paper plates and enjoying a slice while standing in the pizzeria or on the street is a true unique, yet iconic experience for many tourist who visit New York City.

Late Night Memories

I was not a big fan for pizza before I came to New York. If I had to pick between a burger or a slice of pizza, I would always go for a burger. However, after a year or so of being in the city, I started to enjoy eating pizza more and more. A part of that was because of the memories I have created associated with the pizza. Near where I live there are two pizzerias which I usually get my pizza from. There are many evenings when I ended my evening classes and on my way back home, I stopped by one of them and grab a slice of freshly baked cheese pizza as a little treat for myself for finishing such long and exhausted days which hits differently from getting them on a normal day. It is the sense of accomplishments that makes me keep doing it and then stopping by a pizzeria gradually became my little habit that I do almost once a week.

TIGER BALM

Tiger Balm's smell is like a strong mix of menthol and camphor. When you open it up, it's got this sharp, wake-you-up kind of vibe. The menthol is cool and refreshing, like a burst of minty air. Then there's this subtle herbal thing in the background, like a hint of nature. It's not too loud, just hanging out and adding a bit of warmth. The whole smell is like a powerful hit of comfort.

SMELL OF MOM

I have a weird habit; Whenever I get a brand new book, the first thing I always do is put my nose close to the paper and inhale that distinctive scent. There's something about the smell of a fresh book that instantly evokes a sense of comfort and nostalgia. The smell of a book, especially an older one, has a very distinctive and somewhat musty odor. It is a combination of the paper's natural fibers and the ink used in printing. I particularly find the smell very comforting and pleasant, as it connected me with my memories of library.
